THE DEAL

RunGunJumpGun is a tough as nails indie video game that puts a teeth-rattling, gravity-defying weapon in your hands. You’ll use one button to shoot forward, clearing a path, and the other to blast at the ground, propelling yourself into the air so you can helicopter through snaking hallways of spikes, turrets, and saws. Two-buttons worth of terror in one very big, very loud gun.

THE GRIT

With a cast of crazed characters, simple but stupid-hard gameplay, and pulsing musical score, RunGunJumpGun is a loud, trippy mess of twitchy platforming goodness. RunGunJumpGun features warped psychedelic visuals and sweltering beats designed to drive you through this hyperkinetic world. A brutal world bristling with death. It’s not for babies.

THE TEAM

ThirtyThree Games is a tight-knit three-person game studio in Canada. Our first big title, RunGunJumpGun, began as a parting gift to an old workplace we thought would be done in a week. A year and a half of work and a federally incorporated company later, and we’ve rethought some things. The three members split art, code, and sound three ways.
