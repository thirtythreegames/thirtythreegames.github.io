RunGunJumpGun is a tough as nails video game that puts a teeth-rattling, gravity-defying weapon in your hands. With a cast of crazed characters, simple but stupid-hard gameplay, and pulsing musical score, RunGunJumpGun is a loud, trippy mess of twitchy platforming goodness.

Fusing traditional platforming and junky web-browser style helicopter games, RunGunJumpGun revolves around one very big, very loud gun. Aim it forward to clear a path, destroying whatever gets in your way. Blast that thing at the ground, and the force will propel you into the air — letting you helicopter through snaking hallways of spikes, turrets, and saws. Two-button’s worth of terror.

RunGunJumpGun features warped psychedelic visuals and sweltering beats designed to drive you through this hyperkinetic world. Our tight-knit team scored, illustrated, animated and wrote RunGunJumpGun all working together in one room. One tiny, smelly room.

Who we are

ThirtyThree Games is a tight-knit three-person game studio in Canada (in the northernmost major city in North America. Fun facts.). We split art, code, and sound three ways.
